Center for Veterinary Medicine

We help you. Why are we?

We treat little friends with care and attention. Our main goal is to keep your pet healthy for years to come. At the first examination, we will draw up an individual program for feeding, physical activity, care, professional treatment, vaccinations and medical examinations for the animal for the nearest time (until the pet moves to the next age category).
